About Me

Hello, I'm Mai El Sebai,

a passionate baker and artist. My love for baking goes beyond creating delicious cakes, cupcakes, and cookies—it's about turning each treat into a work of art. As a painter, I approach my desserts with the same creativity and attention to detail I use in my artwork. Each cake and confection is a canvas where I can express myself and bring joy to others. Whether it’s a whimsical cupcake design or an elegantly crafted wedding cake, I pour my heart into every piece. I believe that every bite should not only taste amazing but also look beautiful, and I’m dedicated to making your sweet moments unforgettable.
